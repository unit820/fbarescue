6.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies set by the website owner are called first-party cookies. Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called third-party cookies, which enable third-party features or functionality (e.g., advertising, analytics, and interactive content) to be provided on or through the website.

6.2 How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function; set in response to actions such as setting privacy preferences, logging in, or filling out forms
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: Help us measure and improve site performance; allow us to count visits and understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: May be set through our site by advertising partners (e.g., Google AdSense) to build a profile of your interests
  • Functional Cookies: Enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization

6.3 Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Google AdSense, or other advertising/analytics platforms that set cookies on your device to collect anonymous information. These services may collect data like IP address, location, browser and device information, and page views. Third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of those third parties.

6.4 Your Choices Regarding Cookies

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie consent banner. You can accept or reject non-essential cookies at any time. You may also control cookies by adjusting browser settings, opting out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or managing ad preferences through Google Ads Settings.
